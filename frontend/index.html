Welcome to RD Public School Jadagara

Since 2008, we've been providing quality education to young learners in a nurturing and stimulating environment.

Our child-centric approach focuses on holistic development, combining academic excellence with creative expression and physical activity. We believe in fostering curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every student.
